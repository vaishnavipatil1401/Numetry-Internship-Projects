Welcome To
Navjeevan Day School

NDS, CIDCO, a day school established in 1993 in New Nashik. It is English Medium School and offers education from Nursery to SSC. The School is having experienced and dedicated teachers. Recently the school has adopted e-learning i.e. Smart Classes. The school equally provides the opportunities to the students in sports and extracurricular activities.
